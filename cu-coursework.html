Final Write Up

This document should be a reflection processing the course as a whole and the impact it had on you.
In class, we referred to this as the “zoom out, overview”.
We’d like you to address considerations such as:
What career goal(s) did you have coming into the course (think back to your first assignment on your dream career)? How, if at all has that changed? Please specifically address if you’ve changed course and are considering something new (and why), or if the course helped reinforce your goals (and how)? What has this course helped you recognize that you’ll need to do to prepare for this career and how specifically will you need to do that (e.g. raise capital, make a business plan, add to your education, etc.) Which speakers impacted you the most and how? (What specifically did they offer you in terms of advice or what did they say that resonated most?) How did our studio tours help increase your awareness of what kind of environment you’d like to work in? (Specifically, what kind of environment do you see yourself fitting into best and why?) What size company do you see yourself working in? (Is it a large, small company, or do you see yourself going out on your own?) What are the strengths and challenges do you perceive about that model? What were your biggest takeaways from the course? What have you done this semester as a result of this course to advance your professional development that you hadn’t been doing prior? What, if any, feedback do you have for us on the class? Please also add any thoughts or takeaways that we have not specifically outlined that feel important to you.
